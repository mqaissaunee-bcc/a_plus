What is CompTIA A+?

CompTIA A+ is the industry standard for establishing a career in IT. This certification validates the foundation-level skills necessary to perform core IT support functions and pursue IT specializations. A+ covers a wide variety of technologies and vendor-specific products, making it an essential certification for anyone starting their IT journey.
